Bio

Andy Forsberg is a media composer and producer based in Los Angeles. After studying at Berklee College of Music, he began his career working with composer and arranger William Ross before co-founding the video game music and sound design company Hexany Audio.

His work spans documentaries, scripted television, and film, including projects for National Geographic, Disney, BBC, Apple, Discovery Channel, and Lifetime.
